Introduction

Roasty is a Brazilian steakhouse that offers ready-made meals that can be scheduled and delivered to your doorstep via their website and application. The restaurant specializes in serving authentic Brazilian-style grilled meats, including beef, chicken, lamb, and pork, which are cooked to perfection using traditional techniques and spices.

Overview

The Product:

A mobile app for Roasty, a steakhouse restaurant whose target users live in Port Elizabeth and orders ready-to-eat meals very often due to their busy schedules.

The Problem:

Creating an application that allows users to plan their meals and receive them on their scheduled delivery dates, easily.

Goal Statement:

Our steakhouse application will let users schedule meals and place orders in advance which will effect users who lack the time to prepare meals by letting users schedule meal orders in advance. We will measure effectiveness by tacking orders made for 2 or more days in advance.

User Research

1

Not Easy To Use

Other meal ordering applications that allow scheduling is too complicated to use.

2

Lack Of Information

Ingredient lists for meals are not always to to date, are not accurate or is not available.

3

Creating Accounts

Users are usually required to create accounts before checking out and it is tedious.

Takeaways

1. User Research.
Due to this project being for a fictional company, the user research was not done in collaboration with actual users. UX research such as surveys and user interviews would have been greatly beneficial to this project.

2. Team Work
I enjoy the ideas, feedback and joint knowledge that comes with collaborating with a team. For this project, I worked alone. I tried my best to be as open minded as possible but I do think not being able to bounce ideas around would have added more value to this project.
