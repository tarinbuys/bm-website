Your Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge?

The total cost of each project can differ greatly because each client has different needs. Our pricing structure is based on a variety of factors, including project scope, timeline, and complexity. We provide detailed project proposals and estimates to all clients before beginning work to ensure that you sign off on a budget that you are happy with.

How do I get started working with you?

To get started, simply fill out our contact form and provide us with the details of your project. We'll get in touch with you to discuss the next steps.

What types of websites can you create?

We have expertise in developing various types of websites, including e-commerce sites, portfolio websites, blogs, corporate websites, and more. We can use low-code tools such as Divi and Elementor for your convience, or we can code out your entire website if your budget and project timeline allows it.

What do you need from me to start my project?

To start your project, we will need detailed requirements or specifications, any relevant content or branding materials, and access to any existing hosting or domain accounts if applicable. Do not worry about what exactly you need to provide for us, we will send you a checklist and help you as much as we can.

How do I get a quote? How long will it take?

To get a quote, please fill out our contact form or reach out to us via email and you will get a response within 24 hours, excluding weekends and public holidays.

Why choose Buys Management?

At Buys Management, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality websites tailored to meet your specific needs. Our team of experienced professionals ensures attention to detail, timely delivery, and excellent customer support throughout the design and development process.

Buys Management is a UX/UI Design and development powerhouse, based in South Africa.
